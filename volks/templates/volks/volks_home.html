Welcome!

This is a web application proposal for Volks Maniac Garage.
Currently, the app has two users (staffs).

The 'Customer' tab allows for new customer registrations, searching existing customers and searching services.
Each customer has a profile where both staffs can book new services for the customer and view related details such as the number of cars own and the service history related to the customer.
Each car also has a profile which displays the service history related to the car. (Honestly, thats kinda redundant)
When a new service is booked, a Jobcard can be printed once (Let me know if you need to print more than once).
After the service is updated with the related info, an Invoice can be printed.

The other staff has access to more tabs which are the Inventory and Staff tabs.
These tabs may be developed to allow inventory and staff management in the near future.
As of now, the other staff is only able to register new Technicians.
